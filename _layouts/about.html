About Us

ARQ Development Corps, LLC is focused in capital growth in several sectors of the energy industry: exploration and production, energy service, and renewable technology.

ARQ Strategy

  • Focus on single industry: Energy (Oil, Gas, Power, and Renewables)
  • Provide both equity capital and strategic growth assistance
  • Develop private companies into larger, more liquid public entities
  • Increase the value of invested equity by multiple times over a 3 to 5 year time period through strategic acquisitions and internal growth initiatives.

Our Services

Energy Service

ARQ Development Corp, LLC identifies energy service companies with growth strategies in attractive market niches. We target disciplines in the oil field service sector to help energy producers worldwide and companies targeting attractive regional markets such as the Gulf of Guinea and frontier regions around Africa.

Exploration and Production

ARQ Development Corp, LLC sources and invest growth equity and knowledge capital for oil and gas producing companies with an established reserve base and newly-formed companies using growth capital to develop new resources. Its portfolio interest is a focus on niche opportunities such as developing stranded oil and gas resources or developing smaller-scale opportunities in mature operating basins.

Renewable Energy Technology

ARQ Development Corp, LLC is always seeking entrepreneurial technology companies that are developing high-impact technologies that offer measurable improvement in renewable sector.
